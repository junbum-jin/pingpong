푸시는 하회전이 있는 공을 밀어서 넘기는 기술(추가적인 회전X)


A forehand push is a defensive stroke, where you put backspin on the ball. When should you play a forehand push? You're most likely to play a forehand push when the ball already has backspin on it, either from an opponent's serve or in the opening stages of a rally.
